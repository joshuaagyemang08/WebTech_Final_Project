About Us

Welcome to Campus Ride, your reliable partner for safe and convenient transportation. Our mission is to provide a seamless and efficient ride-hailing service for the Ashesi University community and beyond.

At Campus Ride, we understand the importance of timely and secure transportation. Whether you are a student, staff, or visitor, our platform connects you with trusted drivers to ensure you reach your destination comfortably and on time.

Our services are designed with your safety and convenience in mind. We offer a range of vehicle options to suit your needs, from cars to motorbikes, all operated by professional and vetted drivers.

Join us in making transportation easier and more accessible for everyone. Experience the difference with Campus Ride.
